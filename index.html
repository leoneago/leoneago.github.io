I am a population geneticist and marine molecular ecologist working as a researcher at the Department of Earth and Marine Sciences (DiSTeM) of the University of Palermo, Italy. My scientific work is focused on molecular ecology, population genetics/genomics, phylogeography, micro and macro evolution processes of marine organisms with a special interest in sharks and rays. I combine DNA metabarcoding, population genetics and genomics to approach questions on population composition, distribution, structure and connectivity of marine ecosystems.
