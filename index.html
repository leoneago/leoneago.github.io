I am a population geneticist and marine molecular ecologist working as a researcher at the Department of Earth and Marine Sciences (DiSTeM) of the University of Palermo. My scientific work is on marine molecular ecology, marine population genetics and genomics, phylogeography, micro and macro evolution of marine organisms with a special interest in sharks and rays. I combine DNA metabarcoding, population genetics and genomics to approach questions on population composition, distribution, structure and connectivity of marine ecosystems.
